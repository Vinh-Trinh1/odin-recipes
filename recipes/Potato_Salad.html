World's Best Potato Salad

Description

This potatoe salad is creamy, rich, and is sure to give you exactly what you want in a potato salad

ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teasppon cornstarch
  • salt to taste
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 teasppon prepared yellow mustard
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 6 large hard-cooked eggs, diced
  • 1 small onion, finely diced
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. 1. Place potatoes in large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil and then reduce heat and bring to a boil until tender. Drain and set aside to cool.
  2. 2. While potatoes are cooking, whisk together sugar, 2 beaten eggs, and cornstarchin a suacepan; season with salt.
  3. 3. Stir in milk, vinegar, and mustard. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened. Remove from heat and stir in butter.Put in fridge until cool, then stir in mayo.
  4. 4. Peel and dice potatoes. Transfer to a large bowl; toss wit hhard-cooked eggs, onions, celery, and 1 teaspoon salt.
  5. 5. Stir dressing gently into the bowl of potatoe salad until even coated.
  6. 6. Chill before serving, mininum 3 hours and leaving it overnight also works.

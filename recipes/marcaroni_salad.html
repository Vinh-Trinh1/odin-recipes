Marcaroni Salad

Description

This is a simple, easy to make, marcaoni salad that contains nothing but healthy ingredients

ingredients

  • Marcaroni
  • Condiments
  • Sugar and Vinegar
  • Seasonings
  • Vegetables (Celery, onion, bell pepper)

Steps

  1. Cook marcaroni in salted water
  2. Mix together mayo, mustard, seasonings, vinegar, and sugar to taste
  3. Stir in the macaroni and remaining ingredients. CHill in the fridge
